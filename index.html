Viper Panel Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Viper Panel handles VPN/server access and advertising in the app.

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Viper Panel does not directly collect personal information for account registration or personal profiling.

Any username, password, or similar credential used in the app is only for VPN/server authentication and is not treated as personal user data.

Credentials are used only to:

  • authenticate connections, and

AdMob may collect certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, app interactions, and diagnostic information to provide, improve, and measure ads, including personalized ads where allowed.

The app may use third-party services, including Google AdMob, which may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

Viper Panel does not sell or trade personal user information. Some technical or advertising-related data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect authentication and service-related information.
