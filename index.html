Viper Panel Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Viper Panel handles information related to VPN/server access and use of our services.

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Viper Panel provides access to VPN/server services. We respect user privacy and aim to be clear about what information is and is not collected through our service.

2. Information We Collect

Viper Panel does not collect personal information from users.

Any username, password, or similar account credential shown or used in the app is provided only for VPN/server authentication, similar to credentials used in other free VPN panels. These credentials are used only to allow access to the server service and are not treated as personal user data.

3. How Credentials Are Used

Authentication credentials are used only to:

  • provide access to VPN/server services,
  • authenticate connections to the server, and
  • maintain proper operation of the app and related services.

These credentials are not used to identify users as individuals.

4. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal user information, we do not sell, trade, or share personal user data. Authentication credentials are used only as necessary to provide the VPN/server service or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect authentication and service-related information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to improve clarity or reflect service changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.
